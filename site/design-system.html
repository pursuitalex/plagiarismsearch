Hover / active states pass AA

Interactive fills bind to deeper shades so white text stays legible on press.

  • brand-solid-hover orange/700 · 5.37:1
  • brand-solid-active orange/800 · 8.31:1
  • teal-solid-hover teal/700
  • inverse-hover neutral/800

Brand-locked exception

Default orange/500 and teal/500 fills score 2.81–2.90:1 with white text — below AA. Kept per brief §8 because the hues are the brand's core recognition asset.

Mitigation: never use a 500 fill for the only copy of an instruction; hover/active compensate; body text never sits on a 500 surface.

Tracking ramp

Manrope's default spacing is loose at display sizes. Tracking tightens as size grows, and reverses to positive for small caps — the single rule that makes the whole system feel drawn rather than defaulted.

Icons

Lucide, 24×24 viewBox, 2px stroke, round caps and joins. Line icons only — solid or emoji icons break the drawn, lightweight feel. Click to copy the name.

11 — Motion

Motion

Spring-based easing, never linear. Every animation earns its place by explaining something — decorative motion is cut. prefers-reduced-motion disables all of it, with static end states rather than blank space.

12 — Accessibility

The floor

  • WCAG 2.2 AA on all P0/P1 pages
  • Text contrast ≥ 4.5:1, UI contrast ≥ 3:1
  • Visible focus ring on every interactive — 2px teal
  • Pinch-zoom enabled — no maximum-scale=1
  • Color-only signals always backed by icon or text
  • Touch targets ≥ 44×44 · reduced-motion fully supported
